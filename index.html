RETROVERSE

LOST IN THE RETROVERSE: A VR Experience

Role: Lead Programmer @ Retroverse

About: "Lost in the Retroverse" is a Virtual Reality experience that takes you through the grief of losing a loved one and immerses you in the nostalgia of iconic video games like Tetris, Super Mario, and Pacman. Inspired by the most iconic games of the 80s, prepare to be immersed in a virtual world. Lost in the Retroverse is comprised of a variety of creative components: Narrative, 3D Modelling, Game Mechanics and User Interactions, Audio, Graphic Design and Illustrations.
