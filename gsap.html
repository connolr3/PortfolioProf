About Me


I am a PhD student at Trinity College Dublin. My area of research is proxemics and human behaviour in the Metaverse. This position is sponsored by the SFI Centre for Research Training in Digitally-Enhanced Reality (d-real). I have a very broad range of academic interests but some of the mains ones are: AR/VR, Web dev, 3D Modelling & Animation, programming, accessiblity, neurodiversity (brains in general), accessibility and inclusion.
